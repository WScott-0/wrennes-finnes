# wrennes Finnes
Finnes started out as a weekend project to bring my Intellijel case more upright so I could patch more comfortably while sitting at a desk. 

I am making production files available here so that you can help yourself if you also have that need. I am asking that you do not produce these for commercial use. By using these files, you agree to respect the intent of this release: personal benefit, experimentation, and non-commercial making only. I also ask that you if you share photos, remixes, or personal projects using these files, please provide attribution by crediting the original design files to me and linking back to this repository when possible. It is an odd thing, but we are such a small community, a niche within a niche, and Finnes are such a small idea/product, but I am proud of the work and the tiny mark it has left.

## 3D Printing options

Download the .stl file and print. This is obviously not as sturdy as metal, but they worked for me.

## Metal Fabrication

Download the .dxf and cut. Ideal material is 6061 T6 Aluminum. ** Must be .125" to fit the case properly. **

## Additional Notes

For anti-skid and desk protection, I used Silicone Rubber Edge Trim to fit 1/8". You can find this in most hardware stores and online, but usually in long rolls. Skip the version that has 'self adhesive', not work the trouble.

## Original Sales Copy

Custom vertical desktop stands for Intellijel 7u case styles. Finnes insert and lock into your Intellijel 7u case and bring your eurorack case up to a comfortable angle for desktop use. These custom eurorack stand inserts are compatible with Intellijel 7u 84hp and Intellijel 7u 104hp cases and work with handle and jacks up or down. (Right angle cables may be required for inverted use.)
